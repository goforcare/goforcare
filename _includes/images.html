ANGRA DOS REIS
4 DAYS - 3 NIGHTS

ITINERARY

Schedule

Day 1

Sao Paulo

Arrival in São Paulo and transfer to hotel. Accommodation. Then departure for city tour to the main tourist points of the city. Back to the hotel.

Day 2

Sao Paulo - Brasilia

Breakfast, then departure Guarulhos airport to take flight to Brasilia. Arrival in Brasilia and transfer to hotel. Accommodation.

Day 3

Brasilia

Breakfast, then departure for city tour to the main tourist points of the city. Back to the hotel.

Day 4

Brasilia - Salvador

Breakfast, then departure to the Brasilia airport to take flight to Salvador. Arrival in Salvador and transfer to hotel. Accommodation.

Day 5

Salvador

Breakfast, then departure for city tour to the main tourist points of the city. Back to the hotel. Suggestion for a night tour.

Day 6

Salvador

Breakfast. Free day.

Day 7

Salvador - Rio De Janeiro

Breakfast. Transfer to the airport for flight to Rio de Janeiro. Arrival in Rio de Janeiro, assistance and transfer to the airport.

Day 8

Rio De Janeiro

Breakfast. Half-day visit to Sugar Loaf, where you can have a panoramic view of the wonderful city. Back to the hotel.

Day 9

Rio De Janeiro

Breakfast. Half-day visit to Corcovado (Train), one of the 7 Wonders of the World. Back to the hotel.

Day 10

Rio De Janeiro

Breakfast. Free day.

Day 11

Rio De Janeiro - Foz Do Iguazú

Breakfast. In schedule to be informed, transfer to Rio airport. Arrival in Foz do Iguacu, transfer to hotel and accommodation. Departure for tour to Argentinean Falls. Back to the hotel.

Day 12

Foz Do Uguazú

Breakfast. Visit to the Brazilian side of the falls. back to the hotel.

Day 13

Foz Do Uguazú

In schedule to be informed, IGU airport transfer.
