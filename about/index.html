ABOUT OUR AGENCY

We provide the absolute best from Colombia

What can you expect?

Go For Care is an enterprise devoted to offer complex counselling in the purchasing of medical and tourism services in Colombia to people living or residing abroad. We facilitate the decision-making

Our alliances with specialists, clinics, hotels and tourism agencies resemble the highest level of professionalism in Colombia. We are customizing each case according to the needs of our customers, by creating a reliable and comfortable environment.

As a medical tourism facilitator, we take care of each and every one of our patients from the first moment they pick us, accompanying them during the specialist selection, their arrival to our country, during the medical treatment, and their recovery.

Additionally, we support them with tourism plans arrangements and accompanying them during their return home, checking up with them via telephone after their arrival to their home countries. Besides, we provide an interpreter in case it is necessary, local transportation and medical assistance 24/7.

We want to make sure that through the professionalism of our personnel, the charism, cheerfulness and warmth Colombian people are famous for, combined with the breathtaking beauty of our landscapes, our customers receive not only an exquisite medical care, but also the best vacations of their lives.

Guaranteed 24/7 customer support during your stay, so you won't have to lift a finger during your stay, helping your recovery.
