About us

Go For Care is an enterprise devoted to offer complex counselling in the purchasing of medical and tourism services in Colombia to people living or residing abroad. We facilitate the decision-making, customizing each case according to the needs of our customers, by creating a reliable and comfortable environment. In Go For Care we make health services available and affordable for patients from all over the world. We provide you the opportunity to receive a variety of treatments you couldn't find or afford at your home countries, while you enjoy the magnificent beauty of Colombia and our people.

Medical care

We specialize in healthcare. Our alliances with specialists, clinics, hotels and tourism agencies resemble the highest level of professionalism in Colombia. We are customizing each case according to the needs of our customers, by creating a reliable and comfortable environment. Making your recovery smooth and enjoyable is the foundation of our company.

Treatment & Support

As a medical tourism facilitator, we take care of each and every one of our patients from the first moment they pick us, accompanying them during the specialist selection, their arrival to our country, during the medical treatment, and their recovery. While we provide touristic and cultural plans on your trip too, our main priority still remains on giving you the best quality of medical care possible.

Other services and translation

Additionally, we support them with tourism plans arrangements and accompanying them during their return home, checking up with them via telephone after their arrival to their home countries. Besides, we provide an interpreter in case it is necessary, local transportation and medical assistance 24/7. We want to make sure that through the charism, cheerfulness and warmth Colombian people are famous for, combined with the breathtaking beauty of our landscapes, our customers receive not only an exquisite medical care, but also the best vacations of their lives.
