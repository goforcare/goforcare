Choice of specialist: According to your needs we will offer you at least 3 physicians from which you will be able to pick the one feel more comfortable with. Your safety is our biggest concern, therefore only the best physicians will be at your disposal.

Diagnostic: We make sure language is not a barrier for us. For this reason your initial and final medical records will be translated to Spanish and English, if you require us to do so.

Choice of the recovery support team: We count with a professional healthcare team in nursery, physiotherapy, psychology and nutrition that will be at your entire disposal dring you recovery.

Flight tickets: Finding the best options for you is our engine, that is why you will be able to choose from 3 different itineraries the option which suits you the best. In this way you won’t even have to worry about booking your flights.

Inland transport: We will be next to you from the moment you arrive, until the day you leave. All transportation you will need will be provided by us, including transfer from and to the El Dorado airport, the hotel of your choice and medical checkups.

Choice of accommodation: We offer different alternatives for different tastes. Therefore you will be able to choose from 3 hotels the one you like the most. Besides, we will issue an invitation letter for you.

Tourism plan: Let Colombia amaze you with the infinite places it has for you to visit and explore. We offer you a wide list of local and regional tourism packages you can choose from, based on the experiences you are looking for.

Mobile phone: You won’t miss anything that happens at home. We will provide you a mobile phone, so you can keep in touch with your beloved ones. Also you can call us 24/7 in case you have any doubt to be solved.
